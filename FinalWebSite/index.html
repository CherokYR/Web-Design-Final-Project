THE Solar System

The Solar System is a vast and fascinating place, filled with a diverse array of celestial bodies that orbit around our Sun. It consists of eight planets, including Earth, as well as dwarf planets, moons, asteroids, comets, and other small objects. The Solar System is located in the Milky Way galaxy and is estimated to be about 4.6 billion years old. It is a dynamic and ever-changing system that continues to captivate scientists and astronomers alike.
